Colour changer onReload

After meticulously crafting an HTML box and integrating it into a letter, I eagerly awaited the moment to witness its seamless blend within the digital canvas. However, upon reloading the page, a subtle yet striking transformation occurred: the color of the box had shifted. Initially perplexed, I soon realized the intriguing nature of this phenomenon. It dawned on me that HTML, the language of web design, holds dynamic potential. Through its inherent flexibility and the influence of external factors such as CSS stylesheets or JavaScript scripts, elements on a webpage can undergo metamorphosis. This unexpected alteration underscored the dynamic nature of web development, reminding me of the intricacies and possibilities inherent in crafting digital experiences.
